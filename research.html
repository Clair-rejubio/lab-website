Our Research

Our lab is driven by the belief that everyone has the right to the best medical care and maximum lifespan. We focus on understanding and reversing the aging process.

Current Projects

Aging and Epigenetic Information

We study how the loss of epigenetic information affects aging. Our work focuses on identifying reprogramming factors that can reset a cell's epigenetic status and reverse its age.

Drug Development for Aging

Development of small molecules that can treat multiple age-related diseases with a single medicine, including sirtuin activating compounds (STACs) and NAD-boosting molecules.

Mitochondrial Research

Identifying new genes and signaling cascades that control mitochondrial function using genome mining algorithms and high-throughput screening methods.
