Lab Introduction

Xinyang Conquer Aging Laboratory is located in a picturesque, long-life town with a very high forest coverage rate. Our laboratory is dedicated to the study of aging mechanisms and aging reversal solutions led by the legendary scientist, Mr. Bilu Huang. Here, the beauty of nature blends perfectly with scientific exploration, surrounded by lush forests and filled with the freshness and tranquility of nature, providing us with an ideal environment for scientific research.

Our mission is to develop biotechnologies that can reverse cellular aging, cure degenerative diseases, and ultimately, greatly extend human life. We cordially invite researchers from around the world and those interested in rejuvenation science to join our research team, to conquer aging together and contribute to human health and longevity.
