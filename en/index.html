What We Do

Aging Theory

After more than 30 years of research, Mr. Bilu Huang has discovered the secret of cellular immortality. Why cells age is still being explored across the world. He analyzed a variety of worldwide aging theories and found that there were many loopholes and could not be justified. In order to uncover the mystery of cellular aging, he proposed the "Telomere DNA and ribosomal DNA co-regulation model for cell senescence", which passed two years of experimental verification and proved that this hypothesis is completely correct.

Aging Reversal Solutions

Through rigorous logical reasoning and experimental verification, we found that the root cause of cellular senescence and Hayflick limits are telomere DNA and rDNA. To surpass the limitations of lifespan, it is essential to extend telomeres and rDNA. Moreover, merely reversing epigenetic inheritance or modulating signaling pathways will prove ineffective.

The next step will be to use this latest theory to R&D biotechnology to rejuvenate aging cells, which will be used to completely cure various age-related degenerative diseases and greatly extend human life.
